Borrowing from prior works like Mobile Factory, Warptorio, and Warp Drive Machine, comes 'yet another mobile base' mod.

Expect some jank and weird edge cases.  Do not expect me rushing to fix any discovered issue or for it to support your desired overhaul stack of 20+ mods.  I tried to handle the major edge cases, and tested for most crashes.  Multiplayer not tested.  This is why its not even a 1.0 release.

### Gameplay Expectations

You can expect that your mobile base, being a spidertron, enables a fairly quick push and aggressive acquisition of new territory.  You should expect to move around often.  Scouting your next destination based on what you need.

Balance is a rough draft.
